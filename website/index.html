North Italy Switzerland

North Italy: Ortisei and Cortina d'Ampezzo

Our stay in Ortisei was a dream come true. This charming alpine village offered stunning views of the Dolomites, vibrant markets, and cozy lodges. The peaceful atmosphere was perfect for unwinding.

Known as the "Queen of the Dolomites", Cortina d'Ampezzo dazzled us with its majestic peaks and luxurious charm. We loved exploring its trails and enjoying authentic Italian cuisine.

Switzerland: Grindelwald, Interlaken, Lauterbrunnen, and Brienz

Grindelwald felt like stepping into a fairytale. Nestled in the shadow of the Eiger, this village offered breathtaking views, exciting cable car rides, and cozy Swiss chalets.

Interlaken, situated between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz, was a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation. The turquoise lakes and surrounding peaks created an unforgettable backdrop.

Lauterbrunnen, with its stunning valley of 72 waterfalls, left us speechless. Walking amidst the cliffs and hearing the thundering cascades was an unforgettable experience.

Brienz, a picturesque village on the shores of Lake Brienz, enchanted us with its turquoise waters and charming woodcarving traditions. It was a serene retreat amidst Switzerland's natural beauty.
